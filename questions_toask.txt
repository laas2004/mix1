Here are the details about House Rent Allowance:

• The House Rent Allowance (HRA) is paid to employees who do not occupy Company's accommodation.

• The rate of HRA varies based on the place of posting: • For Metro Cities (Delhi, Kolkatta, Mumbai, Chennai): 50% of basic pay • For all other cities: 40% of basic pay

• Employees are required to use Company/Guest House/Approved Hotels wherever available.

• HRA is reimbursed on actual expenses, subject to the company's discretion.
